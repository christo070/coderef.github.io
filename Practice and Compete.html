HackerRank

Hacker Rank is a technology hiring platform that provides a standard for accessing developer skills. This platform is suitable for people from beginners to advanced for learning different skills including programming languages like C, C++, Java, Python, Ruby, Javascript and other Technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Functional Programming, SQL, Databases, Regex, Linux Shell, different programming concepts in Data Structures and Algorithms. It also includes subjects like Mathematics and Statistics and 30 Days of Code,a Tutorial aimed for beginners. It helps the users to practice, learn new coding languages, get Certification for their skills. Futhermore, it helps to those preparing for Interviews by providing a Tutorial named 'Interview Preparation Kit'. Their YouTube channel also features many Tech Videos like Tutorials for Data Structures and Algorithms, Webinars, Live Events, their customer stories and Podcasts.

CodeChef

CodeChef is one of the world’s largest and popular global competitive programming platforms preferred by student & professional programmers. This platform is an excellent pro coders and for beginners with little basic knowledge. It contains lots of coding problems which are divided into different categories such as Beginner, Easy, Medium, Hard, Challenge and Peer. It hosts three featured contests every month (Long Challenge, CookOff & LunchTime). Apart from these, the platform is also open to the entire programming community, especially academic institutes, and student bodies, to host their own contests. Major institutions & Corporates across the globe use CodeChef’s platform to host their contests, internal & external coding assessments. CodeChef’s YouTube Channels are also a source of free learning and knowledge for the community with video editorials and recorded problem-solving sessions from top-rated programmers.

CodeforcesSee for yourself.

Codeforces is a website that hosts competitive programming competitions where coders across the world participates. The contests conducted by Codeforces allow you to write solutions to the problems that are tested during the contest. Project EulerIt’ll blow your mind.

Project Euler is a series of challenging mathematical/computer programming problems that will require more than just mathematical insights to solve. Although mathematics will help you arrive at elegant and efficient methods, the use of a computer and programming skills will be required to solve most problems. The motivation for starting Project Euler, and its continuation, is to provide a platform for the inquiring mind to delve into unfamiliar areas and learn new concepts in a fun and recreational context.The intended audience include students for whom the basic curriculum is not feeding their hunger to learn, adults whose background was not primarily mathematics but had an interest in things mathematical, and professionals who want to keep their problem solving and mathematics on the cutting edge.

HackerEarth provides an enterprise software that helps organisations with their technical hiring needs. It is used by organizations for technical skill assessment and remote video interviewing. HackerEarth Practice offers programming tutorials (data structures, algorithms, math, Python, machine learning, etc.) and practice problems for developers to hone their skills and showcase their profile.
